Accounting starts with recording Financial Transactions through Bookkeeping. Profit or Loss depends on income and expenses, while Assets and Liabilities reflect Financial Position. Financial Statements such as the Balance Sheet, Income Statement, and Cash Flow Statement help evaluate Liquidity, Working Capital, and Financial Performance. Strong Cash Flow Management supports Statutory Compliance and better Budget decisions.
